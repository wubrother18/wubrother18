## 🕹️ Featured Project: Ling Gen Lab

### Ling Gen Lab — Cultivation Idle Game
> **Flutter | Personal Product | Google Play Store**

Independently developed and published a cultivation-themed idle game on Google Play, successfully achieving **1,000+ organic downloads** with a highly engaged player base (**DAU/MAU: 9.56%**).

<!-- Google Play 專屬下載圖卡徽章 -->
[![Get it on Google Play](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/7/78/Google_Play_Store_badge_EN.svg)](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.wu.ling_gen.ling_gen&hl=zh_TW)

### 🚀 Key Responsibilities & Achievements
* **Game & System Design**: Designed complex progression systems including character growth, resource balancing loops, and balanced RNG mechanics.
* **Retention Strategy**: Planned and implemented long-term retention features such as daily missions, a rebirth (prestige) system, and multi-tier resource accumulation.
* **Full-Cycle Product Management**: Managed the entire product lifecycle end-to-end, covering conceptualization, system design, UI/UX prototyping, implementation, deployment, and post-launch agile iterations.
* **Data-Driven Optimization**: Integrated robust data analytics and executed A/B testing pipelines to derive actionable insights and guide product pivot decisions.

### 🛠️ Tech Stack & Architecture
* **Frontend Framework**: `Flutter` (Cross-platform game UI rendering)
* **State Management**: `Riverpod` (Predictable data flow & reactive game state)
* **Backend & Analytics**: `Firebase` (Crashlytics, Analytics, A/B Testing)
* **Storage**: Local Storage (Player progress encryption & high-frequency save states)
